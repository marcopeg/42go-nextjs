# Clarify Round v7 — Enable Email-Only Login With Magic Link and Account Creation

## Questions

### 1. Should successful email sign-ins create rows in `auth.accounts` for provider `email`, or should `auth.users` plus verification tokens be enough?

## Answers

### 1.

Just the user. `auth.accounts` appears to be mostly for social sign-on.

Advice: keep `auth.accounts` for OAuth/social provider links. Email sign-in should resolve through app-scoped `auth.users.email` and verification tokens, without creating synthetic `auth.accounts` rows for provider `email`.

## Resolution Summary

- Email sign-ins should not create `auth.accounts` rows by default.
- `auth.accounts` remains focused on OAuth/social account links.
- Email login identity resolution should use app-scoped `auth.users.email` plus verification-token proof.
