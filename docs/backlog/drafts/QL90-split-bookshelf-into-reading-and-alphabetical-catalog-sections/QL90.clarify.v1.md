# Clarify Round v1 — Split Bookshelf Into Reading and Alphabetical Catalog Sections

## Questions

### 1. How should books that are already being read be prioritized in the bookshelf?

### 2. How should the bookshelf order books for scanning?

### 3. Should the bookshelf stay one mixed list or split into dedicated sections?

## Answers

### 1.

Put currently reading books first.

### 2.

Sort books by title in alphabetical order.

### 3.

Prefer two sections: a reading section first for the book or books currently being read, then a catalog or bookshelf section for the remaining available books.

## Resolution Summary

- Prioritize currently reading books ahead of the rest of the shelf.
- Use alphabetical title ordering instead of the current level-first ordering.
- Make the split explicit with a reading section first and a general catalog section below it.
