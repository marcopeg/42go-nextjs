# Plan — Polish LingoCafe Reader Preferences Popup

## Goal

Polish the LingoCafe reader preferences popup without changing reset data semantics: reset becomes a hidden-when-empty destructive action at the bottom of the scroll content, confirmation protects custom local settings, the mobile preview sticks under the modal header while options scroll, and backlog timestamp tooling writes UTC dates.
