# ZO85 Operator Smoke Checklist

The app is reachable at `http://localhost:3000` in this workspace.

- Log into the default app and open `/profile`; edit account name/image, consent, and save.
- Log into Quicklist and open `/profile`; confirm account, consent, and logout blocks render.
- Log into LingoCafe and open `/profile`; confirm consent and language preferences save through one action.
- Log into LingoCafe and open `/books` with an incomplete user; confirm onboarding collects `ownLang`, `targetLang`, `targetLevel`, and consent.
- Confirm stale or missing required consent keeps LingoCafe onboarding visible.
- Confirm the LingoCafe catalog appears after profile and required consent are complete.
- Confirm `migrate.sql` is reviewed before applying to production.
